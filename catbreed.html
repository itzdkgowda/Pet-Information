List of Cat Breeds

Breed Description
Maine Coon The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds.
Persian The Persian cat is known for its long, luxurious fur.
Siamese The Siamese cat is known for its distinctive color points.
Aegean The Aegean cat is one of the oldest natural domestic cat breeds in existence..
Australian Mist The Australian Mist has the distinction of being the first pedigreed cat ever developed in Australia.
Cheetoh Large, exotic, and active, Cheetoh cats are fun-loving individuals that crave attention from their families
Russian Blue With its soft, silky, short coat in silvery blue-gray, the Russian Blue cat is stunning to behold, but this breed is also a marvelous companion.
